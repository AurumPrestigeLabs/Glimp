**Glimp** (short for *Glimpse*) is a lightweight utility designed to capture the glimmer of an idea before it fades away. Fast, intuitive, and efficient.

## 🚀 Features
* **Instant Acquisition:** Save notes with a single click.
* **Visual Context:** Capture screenshots directly linked to your notes.
* **Organization:** Tag and color-code your ideas for easy filtering.
* **Export Options:** Generate professional reports in **HTML**, **CSV**, or **TXT**.

## 🛠️ How to Use (No Installation Required)
Glimp is a **portable application** written in **AutoIt**. It does not require a formal installation process and does not modify your system registry.

1. **Download** the `Glimp v2.1.exe` (or the `.au3` source code).
2. **Place it** in a folder of your choice (e.g., `C:\Tools\Glimp`).
3. **Run it**: Just double-click the executable.
4. **Data**: All your notes and screenshots will be saved locally in `/logs` and `/screenshots` folders within the same directory.

> **Note for Developers:** If you are running the `.au3` source code, ensure you have [AutoIt3](https://www.autoitscript.com/site/autoit/) installed on your system.

## ⌨️ Shortcuts
* `ALT + SHIFT + N`: Quick text note.
* `ALT + SHIFT + V`: Show/Hide the Dashboard.
